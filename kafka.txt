Copy zookeeper.properties and server.properties from kafka/config to kafka/bin/windows


Start Zookeeper
C:\kafka_2.12-0.10.2.1>.\bin\windows>zookeeper-server-start.bat zookeeper.properties
Start kafka
C:\kafka_2.12-0.10.2.1>.\bin\windows>kafka-server-start.bat server.properties
start console based consumer
C:\kafka_2.12-0.10.2.1>.\bin\windows>kafka-console-consumer.bat --bootstrap-server localhost:9092 --topic my_topic --from-beginning
